Chicken a la king

Description

Chicken a la king, is king

Ingredients

  • 4 portions of rice
  • 1 Yellow onion
  • 250g of mushrooms
  • 2 red or yellow Paprika
  • 500 g of chicken
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 dl of cream
  • 2 1/2 dl water
  • 1 1/2 chicken stock cube
  • 4 teaspoons of cornstarch
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 pot of parsley

Steps

  1. cook the rice
  2. Peel and finely chop the onion. Trim and slice the mushrooms. Split, core and cut the peppers into approx. 2 cm cubes.
  3. Cut the chicken thighs into 4 pieces.
  4. Fry mushrooms and onions in the butter in a large pot. When the mushrooms start to brown, add the chicken. Stir the chicken.
  5. Add cream, water, crumbled stock cube and paprika. Cook for about 10 minutes.
  6. Mix the cornstarch with a little cold water and add to the chicken stew while stirring.
  7. Season the stew with salt, pepper and a few drops of lemon juice.
  8. Chop the parsley.
