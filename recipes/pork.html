Teriyaki-seasoned pork fillet and mushroom risotto

This seductively delicious pork meat seasoned with teriyaki goes just as well in summer with a fresh salad as with roasted root vegetables in autumn or a rich mushroom risotto in winter. The recipe comes from Santa Maria.

Ingredients

  • 600 g pork fillet
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 1/2 tbsp spice mix Teriyaki
  • 4 port risotto rice
  • 200 g fresh sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Set the oven to 150 Â°C.
  2. Brown the pork fillet in butter on all sides in a frying pan over high heat for a few minutes.
  3. Season the fillet with 4/5 of the Teriyaki and then fry it in the oven until the internal temperature is 70 degrees, about 30 minutes.
  4. Let the fillet rest in foil for about 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
  5. Prepare the risotto according to the instructions on the package. Fry the mushrooms in a frying pan and season with the rest of the Teriyaki. Top the risotto with mushrooms and fresh thyme.
